Firefox planning to hinder customization — IMPORTANT PETITION!

🕑: 2015.10.11
✎: 2015.10.11Elliot Wallace

On August 21st 2015, Mozilla announced they will remove XUL and XPCOM support from add-ons in 12 to 18 months. They should not do this, as thousands of add-ons have been written in XUL benefiting millions of users. If this change is to be made, many add-ons that people use will be impossible to create due to the free and open nature of XUL, and many other add-ons have been abandoned by their developers.


Customizing Firefox is why I use it. Restricting that ability, or making it harder to use, would necessitate me finding another browser, or forking Firefox if there are not any viable options

This is an important petition to support if you care about easily configurable software!

https://www.change.org/p/mozilla-don-t-remove-xul-and-xpcom-support-from-add-ons?recruiter=401977396&utm_source=share_petition&utm_medium=copylink
