New compilation demo released! Infinite Universe: Electric Dream Redux

🕑: 2015.05.28
✎: 2015.05.28Elliot Wallace

A new compilation demo is out to promote my upcoming Electric Dream album: Infinite Universe: Electric Dream Redux. With almost two hours of music spanning electronic dance music, new age, jazz-inspired electronica, and more, this collection of older and more recent tracks is sure to provide an entertaining and diverse listen. Two of the tracks are Creative Commons Noncommercial Attribution Share-Alike–licensed remixes, too, so have fun with those!
